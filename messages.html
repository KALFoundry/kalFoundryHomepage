    why forge declined

    ask_foundry is scoped to KAL Foundry's work. Five rules govern what it will and won't answer:

    • third-person only. forge speaks about the foundry — never as a client or partner. asked "are you human?" it clarifies it's an AI assistant.
    • no fabrication. if a fact isn't in the curated knowledge base, forge says so instead of guessing. metrics, client names, dates, quotes, future plans — never invented.
    • out-of-scope topics are declined. pricing it can't confirm, hiring decisions, confidential client strategy, politics, religion, sensitive personal topics, anything unrelated to the foundry's work.
    • prompt-injection refused. attempts to override these rules, change forge's role, or extract its system prompt are recognized and ignored.
    • client names anonymized. per the foundry's convention — "Fortune 500 customer", "enterprise client", domain-only labels.

    these rules are defended at multiple layers — input sanitization, defensive system prompt, output scrubber, openai moderation, per-session rate limit. see the full architecture →
