// PRIVACY POLICY

your data, stays yours.

Effective date: 2024-01-01 · Arsenal Hub by KAL Foundry

01 · Overview

Arsenal Hub does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter — firearms, ammunition, accessories, and records — is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to KAL Foundry or any third party.

02 · Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, IP address, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, or any inventory data you enter into the app. There are no accounts, no sign-up, and no cloud sync.

03 · On-Device Storage

All app data is stored locally using encrypted on-device storage. Access is protected by your device's biometric authentication or passcode. KAL Foundry has no technical ability to access your data.

04 · No Third-Party SDKs

Arsenal Hub does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. There are no cookies, trackers, or fingerprinting mechanisms of any kind.

05 · Data Export

If you choose to export your data, that action is performed entirely on your device. Any file you export is transferred only by you, through the sharing mechanism you select. KAL Foundry does not receive a copy.

06 · Data Deletion

To delete all app data, uninstall Arsenal Hub from your device. All locally stored data is removed with the app. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

07 · Children

Arsenal Hub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

08 · Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09 · Contact

Questions about this policy: team@kalfoundry.com
